📖 About PediPigeon

PediPigeon is a powerful pedigree and breeding management tool for pigeon fanciers. Built with Flutter, it helps you make smarter breeding decisions based on data and genetics.

  • 🧬 Detailed Records: Track sex, breed, ring, DOB, color, and even loan history.
  • 🔍 Genetic Analysis: Calculates optimal distance (Sprint, Middle, Long, etc.) from ancestry.
  • 🧪 Offspring Predictor: Grades compatibility, predicts color & racing distance, and provides expert commentary.
  • 🔁 Simulation Tool: See outcomes of potential pairings before mating your birds.
  • 📊 Pairing History: Review all past mates and their results.
  • 📋 Master List View: Get quick insights into each bird with filters and search.
  • 🌐 Cross-platform: Available on Android and Windows with synced features.
