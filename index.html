📖 About PediPigeon

PediPigeon is a smart, Flutter-based mobile app built for passionate pigeon fanciers and breeders. Designed to help you manage your loft like a pro, PediPigeon lets you:

  • 🕊️ Record and manage detailed profiles for every pigeon — including ring number, breed, color, hatch date, and lineage.
  • 💞 Pair pigeons and track their breeding history.
  • 🌳 Visualize family trees across generations.
  • 🧬 Predict offspring characteristics based on parental genetics — including color and strain.
  • ☁️ Sync your data (cloud-ready) for backup and access from multiple devices.
  • 👤 Customize your loft with user profiles and loft info.

Whether you're maintaining a competitive racing team or simply preserving prized bloodlines, PediPigeon gives you the tools to breed smarter, manage better, and never lose track of your loft's history.
